04 / Campaign Deliverables & Creative Examples
Six pillars.
One conversion funnel.
Every deliverable below is production-ready and live from 01 July 2026. The six pillars feed one another — agent training fills the portal, the portal drives consumer traffic, performance media scales what converts. Co-branded throughout: MGTO crest · Cathay logo · TripFactory wordmark.
A
Agent Training & FAM Trips
"Macau Specialist" certification for 1,500 agents. 4 FAM cohorts of 12 agents each, hosted by MGTO. Booking-tiered cash incentives & leaderboard.
B
B2B Portal Featured Placement
Macau hero card on first screen of 12,000 agent desks. Dedicated microsite, ranking bias in HKG/SE-Asia searches, inventory badging, weekly trade newsletter.
C
Co-Branded Consumer Campaign
Leaderboard, medium-rect, half-page banners + push notifications + emailers. Social (Meta/IG), YouTube, Google Search, programmatic, select OOH at BLR/BOM/DEL airports.
D
Curated Macau Itineraries
4 flagship products — Heritage, Gastronomy, Family/Cotai, MICE. Bookable end-to-end from Day 1 on the TripFactory portal and Nexus B2B network.
E
KOL & Creator Trips
2 waves · 12 vetted creators (travel, food, family). Wave 1 Aug — heritage/food. Wave 2 Oct — family/lifestyle. ~34M total estimated reach. Whitelist amplification 2–3× CTR uplift.
F
Performance Marketing
Search, programmatic & Meta lower-funnel. 40% success-fee structured against booked pax. Weekly dashboard shared with Cathay. Monthly steering reviews. Audited Dec 2026 report.
